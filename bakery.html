Pies

Guatemala, Antigua

San Miguel Farm Washed / Bourbon / 1500~1700m FARMER Estuardo Fadja Castillo Antigua is one of the oldest coffee producing areas in Guatemala and is famous for producing the best coffee in the world. Among them is the Bourbon variety from the San Miguel farm, which is run by the same owner who has won several awards at the Cup of Excellence. It has a strong body, but the bitterness does not follow, and it has a very pleasant taste and a rich flavor coffee.

Insa District, Cauca Province, Colombia

It is a rare coffee collected from farmers around Tierradentoro, a hidden world heritage site. It was known for its high quality, but until a few years ago it has been very difficult to obtain. Enjoy the aroma of coffee and the strong acidity of citrus, followed by the lingering aftertaste.
